# OTIC FOUNDATION - ORGANIZED KNOWLEDGE BASE

---

## About Us

Otic Foundation is a visionary social enterprise dedicated to transforming lives and creating sustainable solutions. We leverage the power of technology, particularly artificial intelligence (AI), to address pressing societal challenges, inspire change, and foster growth in communities across Uganda and beyond.

---

## Mission

To democratize access to AI knowledge and emerging technologies through grassroots advocacy, free skilling initiatives, and community-driven programs that bridge digital divides and empower underrepresented groups for the future of work and sustainable development.

---

## Vision

An inclusive, AI-empowered society where every community in Africa can thrive in the digital age.

---

## Our Approach: Three Pillars of Impact

- **Skilling and Awareness:** Workshops, bootcamps, and hands-on training in AI, data science, and emerging tech.
- **Advocacy:** Engagement with policymakers for responsible AI regulations.
- **Infrastructure Development:** Creation and enhancement of AI infrastructure like labs and innovation hubs.

## Our Initiatives

### 1. National Free AI Skilling Initiative (NFASI)

**Goal:** Empower 1 million Ugandans with future-ready AI skills by 2030.

**Focus Areas:**

- **Machine Learning**  
  Concepts include supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and implementation using Scikit-learn and TensorFlow.

- **Data Science**  
  Covers data cleaning, processing, analysis, and visualization using Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib.

- **Generative AI**  
  Understanding content-generation models like GANs and Transformers, and exploring their creative and ethical implications.

- **Cybersecurity**  
  Training in cryptography, network security, ethical hacking, and risk management.

---

### 2. AI in Every City

A nationwide program offering free AI education. Participants complete a hackathon involving Python for Data Science and Machine Learning, and build projects in Microsoft Power BI. Over 900 applications were received in the initial phase.
